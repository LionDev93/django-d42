Looking for a better HP uCMDB Configuration Management Database (CMDB) alternative? Device42 CMDB is a single source of truth within your organization that ensures that configuration records are accurate with powerful auto-discoveries and provides a clear view into your IT ecosystem.

- Agent-less auto-discoveries keep all your configuration items (CIs) up-to-date.
- Device42 fosters the viewing of unified data across teams as well as departments, creating alignment and consistency company-wide.
- Powerful visualizations highlight the impacts of different CIs on other CIs, making business decisions easier.
- Out-of-the-box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell, and more providing enhanced value from existing investments.
- REST APIs for easy integrations with homegrown and external tools from CMDBs to automation and more!
