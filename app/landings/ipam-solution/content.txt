[landing.title]
Powerful IPAM Solution

[landing.content]
The Device42 IP address management tool is the only solution you need for complete IP, subnet, and VLAN management.
	
- IPv4/IPv6 IP address tool auto-discovery and management
- Complete SSH/WMI/SNMP (v1/v2c/v3) network and server autodiscovery
- Support for overlapping IP ranges and nested subnet management
- Auto-assignment of IPs to any device using APIs and/or integrations
- Automatic correlation of and updates to autodiscovered data!
- Plus DCIM, a CMDB, ... and much, much more!

[feature[1].title]
IPAM Supports Complete Autodiscovery Across All Networks
[feature[1].content]
Device42 autodiscovers across legacy and newer systems, highlighting dependencies between physical, virtuals and applications. Autodiscovery populates your IP address tool with subnets, VLANs, network inventory, switch port mappings.

[feature[2].title]
Overlapping IP ranges with Visualizations
[feature[2].content]
Re-use IPs across different network segments? Device42 handles overlapping IP ranges! Subnet tree and usage charts provide powerful visualization and better operational insight.

Automate IPAM Functions with Device42's RESTful APIs and Integrations
[feature[3].content]
Take advantage of out-of-the-box integrations: Ansible, Foreman, Puppet, Chef, SCCM, Infoblox, JIRA, ServiceNow, vRealize, and many more! Automatically assign IP's in your provisioning and orchestration workflows.
