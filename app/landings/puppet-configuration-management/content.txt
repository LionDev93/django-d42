A Powerful Puppet Configuration Management Database (CMDB)

[landing.content]
Device42 is a Powerful Puppet Configuration Management (CMDB) that Provides a Clear View Into Your IT Ecosystem, Making Puppet Configuration Management More Efficient.

- Agent-less auto-discoveries to keep configuration items (CIs) up-to-date with changes made by puppet configuration management
- Powerful visualizations to make quick business decisions and quickly find impacts of different puppet managed CIs to other CIs
- Out of box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell, and more help you get more value out of existing investments
- REST APIs make quick, custom integrations with homegrown and external CMDBs, automation, or other tools a breeze
