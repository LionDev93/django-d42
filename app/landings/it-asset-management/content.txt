Device42 provides comprehensive IT Asset Management (ITAM) capabilities, including powerful auto-discovery and fully configurable asset types. Easily accommodate all IT asset types across your organization!

- Agent-less auto-discoveries to keep all your inventory up-to-date, with optional agents available for remote assets
- Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure asset management for a complete hardware and software inventory
- Discover hardware and software inventory on all popular operating systems: Linux, Windows, UNIX, Mac, BSD
- Out-of-the-box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell and more for enhanced the value of your existing investments
- Know your support expiry and more with automatic contract management for various vendors
- Easier audits with comprehensive reports and visualization tools
