Device42 provides comprehensive SLM (software license management) capabilities including powerful auto-discovery with fully configurable asset types. Easily accommodate any IT asset type within in your infrastructure.

- Agent-less auto-discoveries to keep all your inventory up-to-date with an optional agent for remote assets.
- Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure to track complete  hardware and software inventory.
- Discover software inventory on any operating system: Windows, Linux, UNIX, Mac, or BSD.
- Out-of-the-box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell and more help you maximize the value of existing investments.
- Powerful visualizations and a brand new reporting engine provide unmatched buisness intelligence from your IT infrastructure data!



[feature[1].title]
Complete Auto Discovery for all networks
[feature[1].content]
Device42 auto-discovery spans from legacy to newer systems and builds inventory and dependencies between physical, virtuals, cloud infrastructure and applications. No need to use multiple tools to put together information anymore.

[feature[2].title]
Complete Software Inventory & License Management
[feature[2].content]
View and manage all software, software license counts, and expiration dates. Manage license keys along with software relationships to devices and users, including complex licensing models like cpu-bound, user-bound and machine-bound licenses.

A Real-time Map of Your IT Infrastructure with Integrations
[feature[3].content]
Go beyond traditional ITAM tools. Enjoy out-of-the-box integrations with JIRA, ServiceNow, Puppet, Chef, Microsoft SCCM, Infoblox, Splunk and more! Use this real-time insight to reduce MTTR and to support decision making across your IT operations.
