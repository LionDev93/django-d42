[landing.content]
Device42 is a powerful service dependencies tool that autodiscovers and maps your hardware, software, application, and service dependencies and interrelationships!

- Agentless application and service dependencies discovery using native protocols: WMI for Windows, SSH for Linux, and SNMP for network hardware
- Service dependencies tool autodiscovers all of your hardware and its service dependencies, including manufacturer and version details
- Discovered service dependencies details include in-use protocol & port information
- Easily view application to application, server, and service dependencies charts and maps
- View comprehensive service dependencies maps even for machines that can't be directly accessed, built out with Netflow data!
