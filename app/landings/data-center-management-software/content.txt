Device42 is a comprehensive data center management software (DCIM) tool that helps you manage and understand your data centers. data center management software features include:

- Drag and Drop building, room, and rack layouts with power consumption and real-time capacity planning
- Map your physical, virtual and cloud networks automatically and agentlessly
- Color-coded room layouts display heatmaps for easy capacity planning
- Fast and light-weight web-based interface, powerful visualizations, and SVG exports that import to Visio
- Resource utilization and real-time power and environmental monitoring with charts and alerts
