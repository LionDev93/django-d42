Device42 is a comprehensive DCIM tool that helps you manage your data centers visually with room and rack layouts, power consumption and real-time capacity planning.


- Auto-generated rack layouts with drag and drop
- Color coded room layouts with heatmaps for easy capacity planning
- Web based with no flash and with SVG exports that lets you import in Visio
- Real time power and environmental monitoring with charts and alerts
- Map your physical, virtual and cloud networks automatically with agentless discovery.
