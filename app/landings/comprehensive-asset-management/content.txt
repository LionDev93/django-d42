## A Complete Asset Management Solution, and So Much More!

Device42 provides comprehensive ITIL asset management capabilities including powerful auto-discovery with fully configurable asset types. Easily accommodate any IT asset type within in your infrastructure.

1. **Comprehensive Agentless Autodiscovery**
Agent-less auto-discoveries to keep all your complete ITIL asset inventory up-to-date, with optional agents available for remote or high security assets or DMZs
2. **Supports for Any Infrastructure Type**
Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure to track complete hardware and software inventory
3. **Complete OS Support**
Discover hardware and software inventory on any operating system: Windows, Linux, UNIX, Mac, or BSD
4. **Powerful Integrations**
Out of box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell, and many more enhance the value of your existing investments
5. **Contract Management**
Know your support expiry and more with automatic contract management for various vendors.
6. **Simplify Audits**
Easier audits with comprehensive reports and visualization tools
