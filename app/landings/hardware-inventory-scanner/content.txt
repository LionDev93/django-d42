Device42 provides comprehensive IT hardware inventory scanner capabilities including powerful, auto-discovery with fully configurable asset types!

- Agent-less auto-discoveries automatically keep your inventory up-to-date, with an optional agent for remote assets
- Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure to track and manage your entire hardware and software inventory
- Hardware inventory scanner works with any operating system: Windows, Linux/*NIX, Mac, or BSD
- Out of box integrations with popular CM tools, ITSM tools, SIEM tools, (and many more!)
- Know support expiration dates, with automatic contract management for various vendors
- Comprehensive reporting and advanced visualization tools make audits easier
