[landing.content]
Device42 provides comprehensive IT hardware inventory management capabilities including powerful, auto-discovery and fully configurable asset types.

- Agent-less auto-discoveries can automatically keep your inventory up-to-date, with an optional agent for remote assets
- Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure to track and manage your entire hardware and software inventory
- Complete hardware inventory management on any operating system: Windows, Linux/*NIX, Mac, or BSD
- Out of box integrations with popular CM tools, ITSM tools, SIEM tools, (and many more!)
- Know support expiration dates, plus automatic contract management for various vendors
- Easier audits with comprehensive reporting and advanced visualization tools


[feature[1].title]
Complete Autodiscovery for All Network Hardware
[feature[1].content]
Device42 auto-discovery builds a complete inventory with dependencies between physical, virtuals, cloud infrastructure, and applications - all with one tool!

[feature[2].title]
Support Entitlement Tracking and Management
[feature[2].content]
Device42 downloads your warranty information from various vendors so you know when your support expires. Know in advance when your server support is up for renewal!


[feature[3].title]
Real-time Map of Your IT Infrastructure with Integrations
[feature[3].content]
Go beyond traditional hardware inventory management tools using our out of box integrations with JIRA, Confluence, Puppet, Chef, Microsoft SCCM, Infoblox, Splunk, ServiceNow, and many more!
