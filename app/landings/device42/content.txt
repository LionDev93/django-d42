Device42 is a comprehensive DCIM (data center infrastructure management) tool that helps you manage and understand your data centers. Features include room and rack layouts, power consumption, real-time capacity planning, and more:


- Map your physical, virtual and cloud networks automatically with agentless discovery
- Auto-generated, photo-realistic rack layouts feature drag and drop
- Color-coded room layouts display heatmaps for easy capacity planning
- Web-based, fast, and light-weight with SVG exports that import to Visio
- Resource Utilization and real-time power and environmental monitoring, reports, charts, alerts, and so much more!
