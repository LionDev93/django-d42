Device42 provides comprehensive IT management capabilities including powerful auto-discovery and fully configurable asset types. Easily manage any IT asset type across your infrastructure!

- Agent-less auto-discoveries to keep all your inventory up-to-date with an optional agent for remote assets.
- Support for physical, virtual and cloud infrastructure to track complete hardware and software inventory.
- Discover hardware and software inventory on any operating system: Windows, Linux, UNIX, Mac, or BSD.
- Out of the box integrations with ITSM tools like JIRA, ServiceNow, Cherwell, and many more!
- Know your support expiry and more with automatic contract management.
- Easier audits with comprehensive reports and visualization tools!


[feature[1].title]
Complete Auto Discovery for All Networks
[feature[1].content]
Device42 auto-discovery spans from legacy to newer systems and builds inventory and dependencies between physical, virtuals, cloud infrastructure and applications. No need to use multiple tools anymore!

[feature[2].title]
Support Entitlement Tracking and Management
[feature[2].content]
Device42 helps you auto-discover your warranty information from various vendors to know when does your support expire. Know in advance when your server support is up for renewal!

[feature[3].title]
Real-time Map of Your IT Infrastructure with Integrations
[feature[3].content]
Go beyond traditional inventory tools using our out of box integrations with JIRA, Confluence, Puppet, Chef, Microsoft SCCM, Infoblox, Splunk, ServiceNow. Use this real-time map to reduce MTTR and make faster decisions!
