{% trans "Solution" %}

{% trans "Using Device42’s powerful agentless auto-discovery process, organizations can create and maintain a comprehensive, accurate map of their entire IT infrastructure — from building to the server, and everything in between. Device42 complements the industry’s most comprehensive auto-discovery and dependency mapping with Automatic Affinity Groups, Resource Utilization, and the Cloud Migration engine to create the ultimate cloud migration toolset:" %}

Successful cloud migration requires that you know all the details about where you are today as well as where you want to go in order to divise the best plan for getting there. Device42 answers the hard questions and automates much of the migraiton planning process by providing exactly the details you need, when you need them at each step along the way.

Device42 Discovers your infrastructure as it sits, including all of the key interdependencies that support your key business applications, and Measures the current resource utilization levels for each critical piece of your infrastructure. Your infrastructure is then Divided into logical “move groups”, each of which includes all the supporting pieces (your target service or application and its dependencies) to run independently. Selects appropriately-sized cloud-based targets for each of these workloads, and compare the price of those target instances on competing cloud vendor platforms.

Automatic Affinity Groups shows you exactly which servers and applications can and must migrate together. You simply specific the ‘key’ backbone service you want to move, and Affinity Groups does the rest. Let Resource Utilization gather RU data on those workloads as they sit for baselining your workloads. Cloud Recommendation steps in to finish planning your migration, using that RU data you gathered and your IT Asset data to recommend appropriate instance sizes and price out your cloud migration on leading cloud vendors!

Next thing you know, you’re in the cloud -- and Device42 stands by your side!
